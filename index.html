About Us

Rose-Hulman Institute of Technology, Cecil T. Lobo Student Chapter of ASCE encourages the development of a professional consciousness, affords civil engineering students an opportunity to become acquainted and to practice working together effectively, and promotes a spirit of congeniality among them, and provides friendly contact with engineering professions.
